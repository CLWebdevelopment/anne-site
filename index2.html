Annmarie's Unique Approach

Why a Health Coach?

Before health coaching I was purely existing.

Being a single mom I gave everything to my kids and nothing left for me. I had no energy . I was binge eating and struggling with focus.

The clothes I was wearing were anything I could throw on without thought, usually sweatpants and sweatshirts or stretchy dresses.My lifestyle was purely work, kids and family never really taking time for my self-care or nourishment.

In 2016 when one of my sons graduated from high school and the other from college.

I told them I needed to be happy.

So we went on a vacation to Disney world.

 

What Changed?

Looking at the pictures I realized how drained and unbelievably bloated I was, I hardly recognized myself. In the upcoming months I realized how disconnected I was when one of my boys tried talking to me, I was so spaced out that he commented “ mom I’m just trying to talk to you”.

 

I made a decision right then to invest in myself to be the best of me.

 

Today I am eating 3 meals a day with foods I love and finding flavorful ways to drink water.When I shared that I was eating breakfast, lunch and dinner with my boys they both said “mom I have never seen you eat breakfast in 20 years.” I am making a conscious effort to take spiritual time for myself and exploring creativity and learning how to create and live balanced healthy lifestyle. My energy level and focus has doubled in this time and I’m at a stable weight.
